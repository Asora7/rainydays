Our Story

Rainydays was founded by Kristopher Larsen. It all started out with him and a sewing machine. He came from a poor family wich made them make this own clothes. He started making jackets for when its raining. He soon got attention for his jackets and people began to offer money to buy is jackets. He made them all by hand and by quality. All this turned into what Rainydays is today. We are a large company that provides jackets made perfectly for keeping you dry and warm. Especially made for the Northern cold weather. Now we have a big staff with thousands of employees. What's always been important for us and that still is very important is that we work as one big family. Rainydays have been making outdoors jackets for 40 years now. We know exactly how to make quality jackets that keeps the wind and rain out. Our story is not over even though we have been around for a long time already. It all started with a single sewing machine.

Quality

All our jackets are made with quality. It’s made from top fibers. We always test out new fibers before we start using them. We make extreme jackets for extreme weather. For jackets to handle extreme weather it needs to be of extreme quality. We have a team that works on our quality every single day. We always want to be better. Even though the jackets have top quality it’s still mid-range in price. We think everyone deserves a comfortable quality jacket. Our jackets are made special for each adventure. We have jackets that’s tailored for hiking, camping, canoeing and skiing. All made from the fibers to fit perfectly your activity.

Our Vision

We always stribe for more. We are to this day located in 7 countries. Our goal is to be accessible for everyone across the globe. This can be done because its online shopping. We want to deliver to every country and have the delivery time no longer than 7 days no matter where you live. Our vision is to be the brand you go to when you need a good outdoor jackets. Our jackets live for many years and will be your partner on your adventures. We also want to expand our line to children jackets as well. It’s extremely important for us that our customers feel like they gained something. We want you to be happy with your order and shopping experience. We want to be the worlds top at making jackets for hard weather.
